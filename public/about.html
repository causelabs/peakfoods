Food is best at its peak.

Peakfoods is a free resource for anyone wanting to think and eat more like a chef. Many of the top restaurants in the world are known for their seasonal menus. Besides just tasting better, seasonal foods also:

  • Have higher vitamin and nutrient retention
  • Cost less as they don’t need to be packed and shipped long distances
  • Reduce fossil-fuel usage and carbon emissions
  • Better support your local economy and local farming
  • Require little to no processing or chemicals to preserve freshness

Origin

Peakfoods was created as an experiment through HiDef, Inc.’s innovative CauseLabs program, an innovative platform for HiDef, Inc. employees to test new ideas that have potential to improve the world.
